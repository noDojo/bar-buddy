## Description

Bar Buddy will make managing your bar tab when you're out for a night on the town a breeze. Not only does it provide an easy way to keep up with your tab, but it also allows you to set your GPS location so you don't ever have to worry about leaving without closing your tab again! Let Bar Buddy keep up with your tab, calculate your tips, and remind you not to leave without paying!

So, let Bar Buddy keep the bartender honest and you just worry about enjoying yourself!

PS - This app does not contain any advertisements!
